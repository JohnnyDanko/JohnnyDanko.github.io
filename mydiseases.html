My Diseases

I was diagnosed with Addison's disease and Hashimoto's disease back in may of 2015. I was found at home unconscience and rushed to the emergency room. I almost died as I was undergoing adrenal failure. The doctors didn't know what was wrong with me at first, but after several blood tests it was determined that I have Addison's disease and Hashimoto's disease.

Addison's Disease

With Addison's disease my immune system attacked and shut down my adrenal gland. Now my adrenal gland no long produces two vital hormones we need to live, one being cortisol and the other being aldosterone. Now I have to take life long medication to replace the hormones I no longer produce.

Hashimoto's Disease

With Hashimoto's disease my immune system has attacked and shut down my thyroid gland. Because of this I don't produce enough of the hormone I need so I have to take medication for life.

Polyglandular Autoimmune Syndrome Type 2

I was later diagnosed in 2017 with Polyglandular Autoimmune Syndrome Type 2. With this disease my immune system is attacking my hormone producing glands. So far my adrenal gland and thyroid no longer work.
